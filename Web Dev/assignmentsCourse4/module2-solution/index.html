Chicken

A very big problem I see with most restaurants is that they describe their menu items with a lifeless list of ingredients. This is unfortunate, because menu descriptions allow you to share the heart and soul of your restaurant with customers and can have a defining impact on a restaurant’s reputation and profits. They really are that important.

  1. Menu descriptions allow a restaurant to differentiate itself
  2. Good menu descriptions entice guests, leading to repeat business
  3. Good menu descriptions lead guests to order more items at a given sitting
